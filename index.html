Khaana: Our Story & History

Welcome to Khaana, a modern foodservice company with a passion for bringing people together over meals. Founded in 2015, Khaana started as a small kitchen in Indianapolis with a vision: to make high-quality, locally-sourced food more accessible.

Our Journey

Over the years, Khaana has grown from serving neighborhood families to partnering with restaurants, schools, and event venues across the Midwest. Today, our team focuses on three main goals:

  • Delivering fresh and sustainable meals
  • Building strong relationships with local farmers
  • Expanding healthy food access in underserved communities

Milestones

  1. 2015 – Founded in Indianapolis, IN
  2. 2017 – Expanded into catering and delivery services
  3. 2020 – Launched community outreach programs
  4. 2023 – Partnered with universities for student meal plans
