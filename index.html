Company Story & History

Khaana was founded in 2015 with a mission to bring fresh, healthy, and locally-sourced meals to communities. What began as a small kitchen in Indianapolis has now grown into a regional foodservice company that partners with family-owned farms near Lafayette, Indiana. Our goal has always been to provide nutritious food while supporting sustainable farming.

Our Journey

  • Delivering fresh and sustainable meals
  • Building strong relationships with local farmers
  • Expanding healthy food access in underserved communities

Milestones

  1. 2015 – Founded in Indianapolis, IN
  2. 2017 – Expanded into catering and delivery services
  3. 2020 – Launched community outreach programs
  4. 2023 – Partnered with universities for student meal plans

Our Commitment

At Khaana, we believe food is more than just a meal – it’s a way to build stronger communities. By working with trusted suppliers such as Fair Oaks Farms, we ensure every box we deliver is filled with care, quality, and sustainability.
